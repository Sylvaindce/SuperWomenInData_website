JUNE 22nd - 2017

We are so excited to announce the first 'Superwomen in Data' event - A series of innovative conferences on digital topics exclusively organized by a group of extraordinary women expert in data and digital practices.

Our objectives are spotlighting women expert in data and digital by giving them the opportunity to share their valuable experience, gathering professionals (of any gender) to discuss the future challenges of digital and data, and raising funds to be donated to a local charity.

'Superwomen in Data' is all this, but also a great opportunity to meet like-minded people during a no-frills gala party where well-crafted cocktails, fine wine and delicious canapés will be served.
